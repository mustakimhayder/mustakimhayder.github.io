Md. Mustakim Hayder

Aspiring Researcher | Laser-Matter Interaction & Computational Materials Science

Welcome
Hello! I'm Md. Mustakim Hayder, a master's student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology, where I also completed my bachelor's degree. My current research focuses on laser-matter interactions, specifically examining how materials respond to high-intensity lasers with ultra-short pulse durations. I'm particularly intrigued by the structural dynamics of materials at the nanoscale under extreme conditions, which are often encountered in advanced manufacturing processes. This work has led me to develop expertise in microscale computational modeling using molecular dynamics.
I'm driven by a relentless pursuit of knowledge, always ready to tackle unfamiliar and challenging topics. This passion for discovery has naturally drawn me towards a research career. My experiences have molded me into a goal-oriented and resilient individual, particularly when facing challenges. I view each obstacle as an opportunity for growth and learning in my ongoing journey of scientific exploration.
