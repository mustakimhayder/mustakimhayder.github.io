Self balancing robot
Our project focuses on creating a self-balancing robot that mimics the inverted pendulum principle. The two-wheeled robot maintains its vertical balance using a combination of hardware and software. We designed the mechanical model based on the state space representation of a cart-pendulum system and implemented a PID controller to achieve a stable inverted position by regulating the pendulum angle and cart position. The robot incorporates DC gear motors with encoders, an Arduino microcontroller, an IMU sensor, and a motor driver. The IMU provides acceleration and angle data, while the encoders measure motor speed. These parameters are used to calculate the necessary force to balance the robot by adjusting the wheel acceleration based on its tilt. We also integrated a Bluetooth module for wireless control via smartphone.
Thermoelectric water cooler design
In this project we utilized thermoelectric module to enforce heat transfer and cool down liquid(water) by creating temperature gradient. For enhancing heat transfer with forced convection a pump is used. Due to lockdown situation we put up this project whatever available in local marketplace. We conducted a sizing analysis of what pump capacity is required to get target temperature drop and a matlab script was written to automate the calculation of pump capacity, given different thermoelectric module specifications.
CBM standard performance chart
As part of the industrial training program for final year students, I was assigned to the production plant of Synovia Pharmaceuticals PLC, where a Condition Based Maintenance (CBM) strategy is employed to ensure a disruption-free production cycle. Our group was tasked with establishing standard operating parameters for CBM by monitoring daily performance metrics, such as RPM of the driving motor, noise level, vibration, and temperature at hotspots of the most used machines in production. The collected data was analyzed for trends and outliers to determine the optimum operating range, aiming to maintain efficient production, minimize downtime, and prevent potential equipment failures. This project provided valuable experience in implementing a CBM strategy and understanding the importance of preventive maintenance in a real-world industrial setting.
